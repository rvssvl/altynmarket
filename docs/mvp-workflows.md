# MVP Workflows

## Customer

1. Enters phone number.
2. Verifies OTP.
3. Browses catalog.
4. Adds products to cart.
5. Selects address.
6. Reviews estimated total with flat delivery fee.
7. Authorizes payment.
8. Receives order status updates.
9. Receives delivery completion notification.

## Picker

1. Receives assigned picking task.
2. Starts picking.
3. Cancels unavailable or poor-quality items.
4. Confirms final picked order.
5. Final total is recalculated.
6. Payment capture is triggered.

## Courier

1. Receives assigned delivery task.
2. Navigates externally with 2GIS or Google Maps.
3. Marks pickup started.
4. Marks picked up.
5. Marks delivering.
6. Marks delivered.

## Admin

1. Maintains catalog, availability, and prices.
2. Reviews live order board.
3. Assigns picker and courier manually.
4. Handles payment/refund exceptions.
5. Manages staff access.
6. Reviews MVP metrics.
